About Heartyculture Nursery
Heartyculture Nursery, located within Kanha Shantivanam,
Hyderabad, is one of the largest and most diverse plant
nurseries in Telangana, spread over 150+ acres. Established to
support the initiatives of the Heartfulness Institute, the
nursery promotes eco-conscious living and sustainable
landscape development.
With a collection of over 1,000 plant species, our nursery
offers:
Native and exotic trees
Ornamental and seasonal flowering plants
Indoor and outdoor foliage
Medicinal, fruit-bearing, and rare species
Bamboo, palms, and timber varieties
Equipped with polyhouses, shade net structures, and
specialized growing zones, Heartyculture Nursery ensures the
highest standards in plant propagation and care.
Purpose:
 The nursery operates as a non-profit initiative, with proceeds
supporting afforestation, meditation centres, public green
spaces, and biodiversity conservation projects by the
Heartfulness Institute.
Highlights:
Rare native species conservation
Innovative multi-grafted plant varieties
Custom landscaping plant supplies for institutional and
public projects
Regular public plant sales and green awareness programs
